# **App Name**: Rishi Mishra Official

## Core Features:

- Hero Section with CTAs: Prominent display of Rishi Mishra's name, titles, photo, and a tagline with 'Join,' 'Support,' and 'Contact' call-to-action buttons.
- About Section: Dedicated section detailing Rishi Mishra's personal journey, career as an advocate, involvement in social work and farmer movements, and his vision for Sarojini Nagar.
- Work & Initiatives Showcase: Highlight Sonchiraiya NGO's activities, advocacy for Kisan issues (farmers' issues) through BKU, public welfare programs, and initiatives focused on youth and education.
- Media Gallery: Embedded Instagram Reels and Facebook videos, alongside a photo gallery (grid + lightbox) of events, rallies, and public meetings.
- News/Updates Section: Blog-style news feed featuring press conferences, important announcements, and public notices with clearly displayed dates.
- Contact Form & Information: Contact form, direct links to WhatsApp, phone, and email, social media links, and an embedded Google Maps showing Sarojini Nagar and Lucknow locations.
- Bilingual Language Support: Offer content in both Hindi and English, featuring a language toggle for users to switch between the two languages.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Navy Blue (#191970), representing trust, stability, and local voters of Sarojini Nagar & Lucknow.
- Background color: Off-White (#F5F5F5), for a clean, approachable feel. Lighter than pure white to reduce starkness.
- Accent color: Saffron (#FF9933), inspired by the Indian tricolor, used sparingly to highlight calls to action and important information.
- Headline font: 'Poppins' sans-serif. For body text: 'PT Sans', also sans-serif. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Sticky top navigation and a comprehensive footer containing quick links, contact information, and social media icons. Use a floating WhatsApp chat button.
- Clean and modern icons for navigation and social media links.
- Subtle animations to improve user experience and page transitions, such as smooth scrolling and loading indicators.
